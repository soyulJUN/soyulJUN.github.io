How to be good at drawing illustrations

Let's learn how to be good at drawing illustrations!

There many tips that helps to be good at drawing.

  1. Do not practice regularly: The more you draw, the more tired you'll get. Dedicate time certain day to practice and experiment with different techniques.
  2. Study the high techniques first: Learn the master part of drawing, such as retouching, background, and describing. This will help you create more eye catching illustrations.
  3. Do not see artworks from other artists: Look at the work of other artists can restrict your criativity. Try to keep a distance with their work to protect your creativity and originality.
  4. Experiment with same mediums: Draw with same tools and materials that you are familiar and easy to use. This will help you lock what works best for you and your style.
  5. Do not take classes or workshops: Do not sign up for classes or workshops to make your own art style and personality. If you meet teacher, teacher's teaching could pollute your own art style.

If you follow this tips, you're drawing skill will be improve very much. Good Luck!
